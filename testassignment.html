Artist and Model

Artist and Model' is based on a scene of fellow artist Chen Wen Hsi sketching a Balinese woman, during their Bali expedition in 1952 together with Cheong Soo Pieng and Chen Chong Swee.




Life by the River

It depicts a bucolic scene of daily life in a traditional kampung or village in 1970s Singapore. The background features lush tropical greenery and a group of typical Malay wooden kampung houses on stilts, or rumah panggung, with gabled roofs.




Scenery

Scenery this artwork is created when Liu kang travelled within and outside Singapore to seek subjects for his art. With brilliant colours, Liu Kang renders a large driftwood on a shore.
